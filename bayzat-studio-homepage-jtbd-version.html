Where it makes a difference

Three things every team is fighting with. Every day.

Bayzat Studio addresses the jobs that drain the most time — the ones that are too manual to scale, too fragmented to track, and too risky to leave undone.

01

Processes that depend on someone remembering

Onboarding, offboarding, probation reviews, visa renewals — all running on memory, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp. When the right person is busy, things fall through.

02

Requests that disappear into inboxes

Employees ask for things via message or email. There's no tracking, no structure, no SLA. HR ends up as the bottleneck — and employees have no idea what's happening with their request.

Data that has to be entered twice — in every system

A salary changes. A new hire starts. Someone transfers departments. And then Finance, IT, and three other tools need to hear about it — manually, from someone on your HR team.
