Leave-Aware Assignment Logic
When an employee goes on leave, Bayzat automatically updates your CRM or ERP to pause new lead or task assignments to that person. The moment they're back, assignments resume. No manual intervention, no leads sitting unattended with an out-of-office employee.
Why it matters
Nothing is lost or left unattended during employee absences — your CRM and Bayzat stay in sync automatically.
