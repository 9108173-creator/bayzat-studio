Introducing Bayzat Studio

Bayzat Studio is the customisation layer of Bayzat's Work Life Platform — a powerful suite of tools for automating HR processes, creating custom apps, and connecting systems. Instead of forcing your operations into rigid software boxes, Bayzat Studio adapts to exactly how your business actually works.

Here's what's possible with Bayzat Studio's four core capabilities:

Bayzat Flows Visual workflow builder that turns your HR processes into automated, intelligent flows with when-if-then logic and 60+ pre-built templates.
Bayzat Requests Customisable service desk with 18+ ticket types, full Arabic and English support, and configurable approval routing.
Bayzat Builder AI-powered app creation — describe what you need in plain language and go from concept to working HR tool in hours.
Bayzat Connect 100+ integrations connecting Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, Xero, QuickBooks and more — no API knowledge needed.
CRM & ERP Integration
Goal: Seamless data flow between Bayzat and your business systems — automated updates, unified processes, zero double-entry.
Automated Sync & Business Logic
  • Leave-aware assignment logic: Automatically update your CRM or ERP to pause new lead or task assignments when an employee is on leave — ensuring nothing is lost or left unattended.
  • Employee data sync: Keep employee records consistent across Bayzat and your CRM/ERP — new hires, role changes, and departures are reflected everywhere in real time.
  • Workflow triggers across systems: Use events in one system to kick off actions in another — for example, a confirmed hire in Bayzat can auto-create a user account in your CRM.
Custom Reports
Goal: Clear insights, data-driven decisions, and complete visibility across teams — tailored to how your business measures success.
Tailored Reporting
  • Business-specific reports: Receive custom reports built around your team's unique KPIs, structures, and operational needs — not generic templates.
  • Cross-department visibility: Combine data across HR, Finance, and Operations into unified views that give decision-makers the full picture.
Organisation Chart
Goal: Full visibility into your company structure — always up to date, easy to navigate, ready to share.
Visual Team Structure
  • Interactive org chart: Access a fully customisable organisation chart that reflects your live team structure — departments, reporting lines, and roles updated automatically as employees join, move, or leave.
  • Downloadable & shareable: Export your org chart in presentation-ready formats to share with leadership, new hires, or external stakeholders.
  • Role & department filtering: Drill down by department, office, or management level to get exactly the view you need.
Streamlined Employee Onboarding
Goal: Faster ramp-up, less manual setup, a consistent first experience every single time.
Before Day One
  • Line manager heads-up: Notify the line manager automatically, so they're prepared and have time blocked to welcome the new joiner.
  • HR & PRO alert: Notify the HR department and PRO to ensure all necessary steps are completed before the start date.
  • IT setup trigger: Notify IT to create email accounts, system access, software licenses, and prepare devices (laptop, phone, etc.).
After They Join
  • Welcome email: Send a welcome covering next steps, tasks, company policies, employee handbook, org chart, and welcome videos.
  • Document reminders: Remind the new hire to upload required documents and complete forms.
  • Team introduction: Send an introductory email to colleagues to introduce the new joiner.
First Months
  • Milestone reminders: Send reminders covering completed milestones and next steps.
  • Onboarding pulse surveys: Automated check-ins at 7, 30, 60, and 90 days to track how onboarding is going.
  • Manager check-in prompts: Notify the line manager to schedule check-ins and track progress.
Auto-Update Employee Profiles
  • Probation end date: Set automatically when an employee joins — no manual entry needed.
  • Line manager assignment: Rules-based auto-assignment based on department or office.
Probation Management
Goal: No missed reviews, clear accountability, documented decisions. Every time.
Before Probation Ends
  • Manager notification: Notify the line manager about the upcoming probation end date and share the performance review form.
  • HR follow-up: Notify HR to ensure the performance review form is completed on time.
  • Calendar invite: Auto-create a calendar invite for the manager and HR, or manager and employee.
After the Probation Review
  • Outcome selection: Line managers select the probation outcome: completed, extended, or terminated.
  • Stakeholder notifications: Notify HR and the employee of the outcome automatically.
  • Platform updates: Trigger the relevant changes — extended probation updates the end date; terminated triggers offboarding.
Streamlined Employee Offboarding
Goal: Controlled exits, no missed steps, cross-team visibility from start to finish.
Before the Employee Leaves
  • Resignation requests: A dedicated ticket type allowing employees to submit their resignation requests directly.
  • Manager handover prompt: Notify the line manager to start planning handover tasks.
  • IT deactivation prep: Notify IT to prepare to deactivate accounts and recover devices.
  • HR & Finance alert: Notify HR and Finance to handle final paperwork and submit End of Service.
  • Employee checklist: Send the employee their offboarding checklist and tasks.
On the Last Working Day
  • Farewell email: Send a guide for returning equipment, accessing final payslips, and other exit steps.
  • Access revocation: Automatically revoke access to Bayzat — no manual action required.
Internal Communication
Goal: Keep everyone informed, reduce risk, improve consistency — without lifting a finger.
Automated Updates & Celebrations
  • Leave updates: Notify relevant teams when someone is on leave — no Slack messages needed.
  • Work anniversary emails: Automatically celebrate employee milestones.
  • Birthday emails: Send birthday greetings to employees on their special day.
  • Public holiday updates: Automatically post on the Newsfeed, send mass emails, or notify via Slack when public holidays are updated.
  • Salary updates: Notify relevant employees when salary information is updated.
